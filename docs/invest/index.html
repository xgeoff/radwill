Commonwealth Invest

Public markets approached with patience and discipline.

Commonwealth Invest serves individuals, families, and owner-operators seeking direct investing access, managed portfolios, retirement planning, and informed market guidance within a more traditional advisory framework.

Core Offering

Brokerage, portfolios, retirement, and guidance.

Client Profile

Affluent individuals and households moving from saving into disciplined long-term market participation.

Platform

A more deliberate investing relationship.

The division is designed for clients who want more structure and insight than a retail brokerage platform, but do not require the full intimacy of Commonwealth Private.

Self-Directed Brokerage

Trading and custody tools for clients who prefer to direct their own market exposure with research and reporting support.

Managed Portfolios

Risk-aligned portfolio models and discretionary management built around long-term allocation discipline.

Retirement Planning

IRA, rollover, and retirement planning services for clients translating income into durable long-range capital.

Advisor Guidance

Portfolio reviews, investment guidance, and market commentary without entering a full private banking relationship.

Retirement & Taxable Accounts

Taxable brokerage, IRA, joint, trust, and business account structures are supported with custody, reporting, and advisory pathways calibrated to longer-horizon households.

Research Posture

Commonwealth Invest emphasizes allocation discipline, market commentary, and patient capital formation rather than high-velocity trading culture.
